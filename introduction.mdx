## About Us

![title](/background.jpg)
Cemetery Studio is dedicated to empowering developers of all levels by helping them learn code and grow their skills in the tech industry. We specialize in creating innovative Discord applications, with a focus on making tech accessible and fostering community growth. Our main projects include Qwenzie, a streamlined ticket bot designed to enhance support experiences, and Grave, a powerful music bot with advanced features for an immersive experience on Discord.
