## About Qwenzie

Qwenzie is your go-to Discord ticket bot, designed to make support management smooth and efficient. Built for communities of all sizes, Qwenzie provides an intuitive panel system, allowing users to open, manage, and resolve tickets with ease. Whether you're managing a small community or a large server, Qwenzie is here to streamline your support process and keep your community organized.
