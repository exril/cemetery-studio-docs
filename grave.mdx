## About Grave

Grave is a powerful music bot for Discord, built to deliver high-quality sound and advanced features for an exceptional audio experience. Perfect for communities of all sizes, Grave offers seamless music streaming and easy controls, so you can enjoy music without hassle. Whether you’re running a cozy server or a bustling community, Grave enhances your server’s music experience and keeps everyone entertained.
